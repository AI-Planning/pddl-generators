Usage:
  depots -e <#depots> -i <#distributors> -t <#trucks> -p <#pallets> -h <#hoists>
         -c <#crates> [-s <random_seed>]

        All numbers are positive integers (minimal 1).


IPC instances:
depots from 1 to 6
distributors from 2 to 6
trucks from 2 to 6
pallets from 3 to 20
crates from 2 to 20
hoists from 3 to 15
